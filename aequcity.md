## **2 │ MASTER LAYOUT — CONCENTRIC-RADIAL-SECTORAL HYBRID**

### 2.1 — Why This Shape?

After iterating through grid, linear, radial, concentric, hexagonal, and organic layouts:

- **Grid** — efficient but ignores terrain/wind/sun
- **Linear** — good for transport but poor access equity
- **Radial** — good centrality but wastes edge zones
- **Hexagonal** — most space-efficient tessellation but complex construction
- **Concentric-Radial-Sectoral Hybrid** — ✅ **OPTIBEST** — combines equidistant access (concentric), directional zoning (sectoral), and transport efficiency (radial), while adapting to terrain
