**SOD × Selenium/GPx (Ensure) × Chaga (SOD-mimetic melanin) × Ergothioneine × Astaxanthin × Copper/Zinc/Mn (Ensure SOD metallocofactors)**

SOD dismutates O₂•⁻ → **H₂O₂** — which is *still toxic.* H₂O₂ must be cleared by Glutathione Peroxidase (GPx) or Catalase:

```
O₂•⁻ → [SOD] → H₂O₂ → [Se-GPx] → H₂O
```

Without **Selenium** (the GPx enzyme cofactor provided by Ensure Gold), supplemental SOD generates H₂O₂ accumulation — substituting one ROS for another. Additionally:
- Cu/Zn/Mn from Ensure Gold are the metallocofactor cores of Cu/Zn-SOD and Mn-SOD isoforms
- Chaga's melanin complex acts as an electron sink for residual radicals
- Ergothioneine scavenges in the organelle space
- Astaxanthin handles lipid peroxidation propagation in membranes

**Combination-only effect:** SOD supplementation is incomplete and potentially counterproductive without adequate selenium. Ensure Gold closes this gap automatically, completing the full O₂•⁻ → H₂O elimination chain.
