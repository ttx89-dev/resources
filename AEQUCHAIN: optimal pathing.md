## I. FOUNDATIONAL DEFINITIONS

**Complete Network Internalization (CNI):** The process by which every productive function in an economy — from raw material extraction to final service delivery — is absorbed into the AequChain network, replacing profit-extractive intermediaries with equidistributed protocol-governed coordination.

**The EVER Model:**
- **E**quidistribution — Value flows equally to all participants by protocol, not by power
- **V**alidation — Every transaction, contribution, and resource flow is chain-verified
- **E**xecution — Smart-contract-driven operations replace managerial hierarchy
- **R**ecirculation — All surplus re-enters the network; no extraction, no hoarding

**The Goal:** Every business, in every industry, becomes a **Network Node** — not a private enterprise. Ownership dissolves into participation. Profit dissolves into distribution.
